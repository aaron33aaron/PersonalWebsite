Education

I started my studies at Frenchman's Bay public school where I completed my studies in English and French which has granted me the benefit of being bilingual. I then attended Dunbarton Highschool where I explored my interests and developed a passion for the power that technology holds and the benefits it could bring to people across the world. This lead me to enrolling in Game Development and Entrepernership at Ontario Tech University where I furthered my soft and hard skills, eventually switching over to the Durham College Computer Programming and Analysis program where I have made great connections in several industries and furthered my skills such as database development, front-end development, back-end development and working with clients to reach their vision for their products. I have completed the University of Helsinki's Element's of AI course which has furthered my knowledge and skills when working with machine learning, neural networks, the philosophy of artificial intelligence, and using artificial intelligence to solve problems. I have also completed the ConsenSys Academy Blockchain Foundations and Use Cases course which has expanded my knowledge on the fundamentals of how blockchains work, their uses and how and when they should be implemented.
